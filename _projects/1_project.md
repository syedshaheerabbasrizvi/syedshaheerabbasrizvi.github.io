Designing a 4-axis gantry system to automate microwave packaging and palletising at Dawlance, targeting a reduction in NIOSH Lifting Index from 3.1 to below 1.0 and cycle time from 30s to under 25s. Developing a vision-guided control architecture integrating object localisation, pose estimation, and motion synchronisation for autonomous pick-and-place. Redesigned task allocation under Industry 5.0 principles, preserving human roles in dexterous and judgment-based operations.

**Supervisor:** Dr. Abdul Basit Memon — Habib University × Dawlance
