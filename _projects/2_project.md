Co-developed a two-stage multimodal MobileViT pipeline achieving 98.86% crop classification and 96.62% disease detection accuracy across 16 crops and 54 disease classes. Curated a Unified Plant Disease Dataset (67,166 images) from four geographically diverse sources. Implemented GradCAM++, Monte Carlo Dropout, and counterfactual testing for explainability and robustness.
